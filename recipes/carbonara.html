Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Delicious and easy to make, the original pasta carbonara from its italian origin. No cream, no extra toppings. The classic dish with eggs, pancetta and parmesan cheese. Hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

For 4 people

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta (you can use bacon instead)
  • Olive oil
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • 4 eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Add a generous amount of salt to a pot filled with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add your spaghetti to the water, close the lid (if you have one) and let them sit until the water boils again. After that, switch to medium heat and stir ocasionally.
  3. Add two smashed cloves of garlic and the diced pancetta to a pan with a tablespoon of olive oil and let it cook at medium heat.
  4. Once the garlic is brown, remove it - and give yourself a chef's treat, if you want ;)
  5. For the sauce, throw a whole egg, three egg yolks and grated parmesan cheese into a bowl. Add a pinch of pepper and a pinch of salt and mix it all.
  6. Once the pancetta has a decent color, add the pasta to the pan with a good amount of pasta water and remove the pan from the heat.
  7. Add the sauce to the pan, and, optionally, a tablespoon of butter for more creaminess. Mix it all.
  8. Finally, present the dish with some more parmesan cheese and pepper on top, and you're done!
