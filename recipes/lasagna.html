Lasagna

Description

A recipe for the whole family. A simple yet mouth-watering classic lasagna.

Ingredients

For 4 people

  • 100 grams of unsalted butter
  • 80 grams of flour
  • 1 liter of milk
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • A quarter of a nutmeg
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ragu (you can make it yourself or buy some)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Aluminium foil

Steps

Bechamel (White Sauce)

  1. Add your butter to a big pot at medium heat and let it melt.
  2. Throw the flour to the pot, stir it constantly to avoid lumps.
  3. Pour in the milk and 100 grams of grated parmesan cheese. Don't stop stiring.
  4. Add a pinch of salt, a pinch of pepper and grated nutmeg.
  5. Swap to a whisk and whisk the mix for about 10 minutes on low heat.

Lasagna

  1. On a large rectangular recipient, start by adding a bit of Ragu on the bottom layer.
  2. Add the first lasagna sheet, then add ragu, bechamel, mozzarella and grated parmesan.
  3. Repeat for as many layers you want (or as many as the container can fit!)
  4. Cover the recipient with aluminium foil and put it in the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 35 to 45 minutes. Remove the aluminium foil for the last 5 minutes. That's it!
